# OpenMusE – Hospitality Survey (Survey Panel Version, Task 1.3)

This repository contains the research materials underlying the empirical analysis presented in **OpenMusE Deliverable D1.3**.

The data stem from the *OpenMusE Hospitality Survey Distributed Through A Survey Panel Provider (v1.0)*.

---

## Repository Contents

### Machine-Readable Metadata

- `OpenMusE T1.3 Hospitality Survey Distributed Through A Survey Panel Provider full.xml`  
  Structured metadata file describing survey design, variables, coverage, and methodology.

- `OpenMusE_T1.3_Hospitality_Survey_Survey_Panel_codebook_v1.0.xlsx`  
  Variable-level codebook including response categories and routing logic.

### Survey Data

Combined dataset (Germany, Italy, Poland):

- `OpenMusE_T1.3_Hospitality_Survey_Survey_Panel_data_DE_IT_PL.csv`
- `OpenMusE_T1.3_Hospitality_Survey_Survey_Panel_data_DE_IT_PL.sav`

Countries covered:

- Germany (DE)
- Italy (IT)
- Poland (PL)

Fieldwork period: 05 August 2025 – 11 September 2025  
Survey mode: Online self-completion  
Sampling: Quota sample via professional survey panel provider (Dynata)

Final achieved sample:  
N = 601 (DE = 230; IT = 150; PL = 221)

## Scope of the Survey

The survey targets professionals working in hospitality venues (restaurants, bars, cafés, hotels).

It covers:

- Employment characteristics  
- Workplace characteristics  
- Background music usage and licensing  
- Music selection and provision  
- Organization and frequency of live music events  
- Revenue effects and perceived economic impact of music  

The empirical results based on these data are reported in **OpenMusE Deliverable D1.3**.
